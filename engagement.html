Volunteering

Since 2018, I have been volunteering with the German Olympic Sports Confederation (DOSB), supporting major events such as athlete gatherings, Olympic team activations, and national celebrations. I remain active in this role and plan to continue through 2026.

I also coach competitive ski racing (U8–U12) at Ski Club Wolfratshausen. I developed and led the club’s digital media strategy until 2025, significantly increasing visibility and attracting sponsors. In 2024, the club was awarded 'Ski Club of the Year' for its digital approach. The same year, I received the 'Sports Promoter Award' from the city of Wolfratshausen.
